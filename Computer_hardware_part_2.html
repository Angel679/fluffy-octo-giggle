The Motherboard itself
is a printed circuit board
that allows the CPU, RAM
, and all other computer hardware components
to communicate with each
other. There are huge
variations in Motherboards
from design, cases, power
supply, size (Form Factor)and
compatibility

The central processing
unit (CPU) is the brain
of your computer. It
handles all the instructions
you give your computer,
and the faster it does this,
the better. Learn about how
a CPU processes instructions
and how computer engineers
are continuously coming up
with ways to make it go faster.

RAM - Random Access
Memory, or volatile
memory, is used by
the system to store data
for processing by a
computer's
central processing unit
(CPU), also known as
the processor.
