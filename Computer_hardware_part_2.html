Hard Drive

Storage is the hard drive's responsibility. Everything you keep on your computer is on a hard drive. Not just documents, pictures, music, and videos

Computer components are connected to each other via thin connections known as 'bus' on the motherboard. The devices follow a communication protocol defined by IEEE. Protocols tend to differ for each type of device / bus. Since multiple devices can be connected to the same bus, the communications are handled by the 'bus controller'.
